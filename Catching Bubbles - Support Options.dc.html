Support Options

Care that meets you
where you are

Every family's village looks a little different. Whether you need someone by your side through the nights, an extra set of hands during the day, or help pulling the whole village together, there's a way for us to hold you.

Our Specialty

Overnight & Newborn Care

This is where we live. Our overnight & newborn specialists take the nights so you can sleep, heal, and wake up ready to love on your baby instead of running on empty. Feedings, soothing, swaddling, gentle sleep support — we handle the dark hours with calm, practiced hands.

What's included
Overnight feedings & soothing
Newborn care coaching
Gentle sleep shaping
Overnight logs for pediatric visits
Flexible 8–12 hour shifts

Postpartum Doula Care

Daytime, hands-on support for the fourth trimester. Your doula moves through your home doing whatever the day actually calls for — holding the baby so you can shower, prepping a warm meal, or simply sitting with you while your body and heart catch up to everything that's changed.

What's included
Newborn & infant care support
Light meal prep
Emotional & physical recovery support
Sibling & household rhythm help

Birth Doula Support

Steady, continuous care from the last weeks of pregnancy through labor and delivery. Your birth doula helps you build a plan you feel good about, then stays close through every contraction — offering comfort measures, calm guidance, and an anchor in the room, wherever you're birthing.

What's included
Prenatal planning visits
On-call from 38 weeks
Continuous labor support
Partner coaching
One postpartum follow-up visit

Lactation Support

Feeding your baby shouldn't feel like a fight. Our lactation specialists sit with you through latch troubles, supply questions, pumping schedules, and everything in between — with zero judgment about however you decide to feed your family.

What's included
In-home latch & positioning help
Supply & weight-gain guidance
Pumping & bottle-feeding plans
Judgment-free combo & formula support

Family Coordination

You have a village — grandparents, friends, meal trains, well-meaning offers to help. Our coordination support gathers all of it into one gentle, organized plan, so care actually reaches you instead of getting lost in good intentions.

What's included
Care calendar & scheduling
Meal train & visitor coordination
Vendor & appointment tracking
One point of contact for your village

Twin & Multiples Care

Two (or more) babies call for a different kind of village. This is Jordan's specialty on our team — staggered feeding schedules, tandem soothing, and the extra set of hands that makes multiples feel manageable instead of overwhelming, night after night.

What's included
Staggered feeding & sleep systems
Multiples-experienced specialists
Extra overnight coverage options
Guidance from Jordan's specialty team
