FAQ

Questions, answered

How much time does the program take?

Most trainees complete the full pathway in 8–12 weeks, working through coursework at their own pace alongside a handful of scheduled mentorship shifts.

Do I need prior experience?

No formal experience is required — just warmth, reliability, and a genuine love for supporting new families. Doula, nursing, or childcare backgrounds are a plus, not a must.

What does certification cost?

Tuition is discussed during your application call, and flexible payment plans are available. Many specialists find their first placements more than cover the investment.

Will I be placed with families after I'm certified?

Yes. Certified specialists join our active roster and are matched with families through 4 Haven based on availability, location, and fit.

What happens after I'm certified?

You'll join our 4 Haven community of specialists, with continuing education, peer support, and ongoing mentorship as you grow your caseload.
